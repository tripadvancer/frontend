# How to find a random place around you

Would you like to find a random place nearby? Visit our [map](https://www.tripadvancer.me/maps) page and switch to the 'Random' tab on the widget.

[there are image]

Before using this feature, make sure your location is detected. You can do this in two ways:

1) Press the location detection button on the map or in the widget.
2) Click on the map approximately where you are, then in the popup window, click "I am here".
