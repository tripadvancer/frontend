    ##### What are Cookies?
    
    Cookies are small text files that are placed on&nbsp;your device (e.g., computer,
    smartphone, or&nbsp;tablet) when you visit a&nbsp;website. They are widely used to&nbsp;make websites work
    or&nbsp;to&nbsp;improve their efficiency, as&nbsp;well as&nbsp;to&nbsp;provide information to&nbsp;the website
    owners.
</article>
